Family Trip to London - 16th August 2022

We're just back from a family trip to London, we had a great time. With the exception of this afternoon the sun was shining as it has been across the UK for the past week or so. The hotel air con was a nice bonus after a warm day in the sun and had great views of the Tower of London and Tower Bridge. Both were a five minute walk from our hotel.

We spent two hours on a Peppa Pig bus tour on Monday. The girls enjoyed it plus the sandwiches and cakes were good. It gave us nice views of The Shard, Big Ben, St. Paul's and a few other must see attractions. Tuesday morning we headed over to the West End and watched Room on the Broom. It's a book we have read many times with both girls and we all enjoyed the show.

As for the London Underground, it's an amazing piece of engineering and makes the city so much more accessible. Unless you have a pram and several bags to deal with which means carrying them up and down a lot of stairs. We got lucky with a few stations being accessible. And we got where we needed to go.

This evening we have two very tired girls and two very tired parents. It will be an early night all around.
