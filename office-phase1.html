New Home Office Phase 1 - 1st August 2022

With baby number three on the way and due in October I'll be losing my home office (the spare bedroom). I spent Friday emptying our garage so that I could paint the floor ready to start my new office build. Luckily, my hopes for a dry day were realised. The paint will stop concrete dust kicking up every time I brush out leaves that make it under the door. Plus it looks better.

The first coat went on well and dried quickly. The second coat was similar but I had to start putting everything back before it had "set hard". The end result was fine, albeit with some marks. With half of it covered by the new office floor and the other half likely taking damage as a working garage, I'm not worrying about it.

The next phase is getting an internal wall up to divide the new office from what will be the reduced garage. I'll be paying someone to do that for me.
