New Home Office Phase 1 - 1st August 2022

Re-written on 3rd August 2022

I spent Friday emptying our garage so I could paint the floor. The main reason is to stop the concrete dust kicking up when I brush out the leaves. We will also be taking half the space for my new home office. With our third baby on the way and due in October the current office will become a bedroom.

The job itself went well. With the contents of the garage outside I was fortunate to avoid any rain and got a couple of coats on before putting everything back. The next bit will be done by a professional, hopefully in the next two to three weeks.
